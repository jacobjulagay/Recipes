In N Out Burger

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics.

Hamburger Ingredients

  • Hamburger
  • Buns
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Yellow Mustard

Special Sauce Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Sweet Pickle Relish
  • White vinegar

How to Carmelize Onions

  1. Finely chop your onion and add into a frying pan with oil. Stir and saute for a few minutes.
  2. Cover them and allow them to cook slow for about 30 minutes until they are golden and soft. Watch your heat and donâ€™t let them burn.
  3. Uncover them once they have started to brown and caramelize and cook for about 5 more minutes uncovered.
  4. Add in 1/2 C water and simmer until the water evaporates.

Making the burger

  1. Toast the Buns. This is one of the things I love about an In-N- Out Burger! Heat up your electric griddle and brush it lightly with some vegetable oil. Place your buns split side down on the griddle and toast until golden brown.
  2. Form your hamburger patties. I made my patties thicker than In-N-Out, but you can make them thinner for a more authentic experience. Add a sprinkle of salt and pepper to both sides.
  3. Place your patties on your hot griddle (these hamburgers are not put on a BBQ grill)
  4. Cook for 3-5 minutes, depending on the thickness of your patty and your desired doneness.
  5. Spread the patty with 1 1/2 tsp of mustard on the uncooked side and then flip
  6. Top the cooked side with a slice of cheese. In-N-Out uses American cheese but I used a nice slice of cheddar. Again, make it with the cheese you love, or use American for an authentic In-N-Out burger!
  7. After cooking to your desired doneness you are ready to assemble your burger
  8. Spread some of the burger sauce on your bun.
  9. Add lettuce, tomato, hamburger patty and caramelized onions.
